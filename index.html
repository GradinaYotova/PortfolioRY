Hello World ¦⁆
My name is Radina Yotova. I am an interactive designer based in Sofia / The Hague. I've graduated a bachelor degree in Graphic Design in Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.
Mostly I am interested in image-making and storytelling, produced and designed by means of new media technology. Some of the topics I am passionate about are social behavior, participatory culture, network culture and computer vision. I love to experiment, always learn more and keep the playfulness in my work.
